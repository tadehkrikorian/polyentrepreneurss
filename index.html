Ember Alert: MIT Finalist

Ember Alert is an award-winning wildfire defense system designed by Poly Entrepreneurs. The device is a compact, low-cost fire detection module that uses a network of BLE-connected sensors to detect heat, gas, and humidity. When deployed across neighborhoods, it forms a mesh network that communicates fire threats in real time and activates suppression systems like sprinklers and retardants. It was selected as a finalist in the MIT LaunchX entrepreneurship competition.
